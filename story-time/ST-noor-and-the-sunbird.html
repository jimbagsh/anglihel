Noor and the Sunbird

__________

One day, Noor was sitting at home. Her mother was embroidering a shirt and Noor was bored.

“Mommy is working hard on my shirt.
I wonder, where can I find pretty flowers to get her something nice.”

Then from the sky a small bird swoops down on the windowsill.
Its beak was curved, and its feathers were blue, green and purple like precious gems.
Noor was amazed at this bird’s beauty.
“What’s your name?”

“My name is Tamir and I’m a Palestine Sunbird.”
“I hear you’re looking for pretty flowers. I know where you can find them!”

“The prettiest poppies and irises grow under my home in a cypress tree.”
“The sun always shines on them.”
“Why don’t I take you there?”

Noor agreed right away, bid farewell to her mum and left with Tamir.

Noor and Tamir followed the rays of the sun until they found the tree they were looking for.
The cypress tree and all the flowers around it shone brightly under the sun’s light.

Noor and Tamir picked the flowers and tied them together with some string.

“Thank you for helping me find pretty flowers, oh kind Tamir!”
“You’re very welcome dear, and thank you for the company oh sweet Noor!”

With that, Noor went back home with her bouquet of flowers.
“Surprise! Look what I got you mommy!”

“Oh my! These flowers are so pretty! You’re so kind my sweet daughter.”
“Here, I just finished embroidering your shirt!”

“I even added your favorite flower!”
“Yay! I love it so much! You’re the best mom!”

  • precious: rare and worth a lot of money
  • embroidering: to decorate cloth with a pattern of stitches usually using coloured thread
  • poppies: a wild or garden plant, with a large delicate flower that is usually red
  • irises: a tall plant with long pointed leaves and large purple or yellow flowers
  • cypress: a tall straight evergreen tree
  • bouquet: a bunch of flowers arranged in an attractive way so that it can be carried in a ceremony or presented as a gift
