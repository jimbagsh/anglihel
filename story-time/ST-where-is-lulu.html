Where is Lulu?

Read by Zoe Aloise

Where is Lulu and why is she hiding?

“Oh, no!”

“Lulu! It’s time to go,” calls Ma.

Where is Lulu?
Is she under the sofa?
Is she behind the curtain?

Where is Lulu?
Is she next to the stove?
Is she in the cupboard?

“Lulu! It’s time to go. Where are you?” calls Ma.

“There you are!”
“I don’t want to gooo!”

“We have to go,” says Ma.
“But I really like this one!”

“I really like this one!”

“And this one.”
“And this one.”
“And this one.”

“See? You return books and borrow more.”
“YAY!”

Where is Lulu now?

  • sofa: a long comfortable seat with a back and arms, for two or more people to sit on
  • stove: something for cooking food, containing gas or electric rings on top
  • cupboard: a piece of furniture with doors and shelves used for storing dishes, food, or clothes
  • borrow: to take and use something that belongs to somebody else, and return it to them at a later time
  • curtain: a piece of cloth that is hung to cover a window
