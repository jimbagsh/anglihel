This cute little baby knows exactly what she wants. She wants to eat, she wants to play, and she wants to read a book with Mama!

I want to crawl.

I want to climb.

I want to eat.

I want to drink.

I want to sing.

I want to play.

I want to draw.

I want to splash.

I want to go to Mama.

I want to read.

I want to hug.

I want to sleep.
