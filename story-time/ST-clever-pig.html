Clever Pig

Read by Jim Damico @ Anglihel.com

Pig loves to play! Clever Pig. One day, Pig is gone. Will Pig come back?

Come Pig! Let’s play.
Can you find the carrot?

No, it’s not under there.
No, it’s not inside.

Clever Pig!

Wake up, Pig! Come out and play.
Where is she?

Pig is gone.

The sun came up.
And the sun went down.
But, no Pig.

I miss my friend.

My Pig! Clever Pig.

And one, two, three new friends!

Come, pigs!
Let’s play.

Time for bed!

Goodnight, pigs.
