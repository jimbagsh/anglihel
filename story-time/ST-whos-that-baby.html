Who's that Baby?

Read by Mika Ulmet

A new baby has come to play. Who could it be?

Who’s that baby?

That baby can walk.
Just like me.

My wiggly toes.
That baby’s wiggly toes!

Oh? That baby’s gone!

Who’s that baby?

My waving hands.
That baby’s waving hands.

Want to play?

Oh, no! That baby’s gone!

Who is that baby?

My cute nose.
That baby’s cute nose.

I make a funny face.
So does that baby.

Oh! That baby is... ME!
