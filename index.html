About Anglihel.com

Hello, my students call me 'Jim bagsh' because I taught English as a foreign language in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia. I started this website so that we could reach even more people throughout Mongolia and beyond.

Besides working as an EFL teacher in Mongolia, I have also worked as an EFL Teacher Trainer in Thailand, Mongolia and now in Nepal helping teachers and students improve their English fluency.

This site is for anyone who wants to learn English. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, feel free to contact me on the Anglihel.com Facebook page.
