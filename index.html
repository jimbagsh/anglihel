About Anglihel.com

Hello, my students call me 'Jim bagsh' because I taught English as a second language at a Buddhist center in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia. I started this website so that our English department could reach even more people throughout Mongolia and beyond.

After working as an ESL Teacher Trainer in Thailand for the last three years, I have returned to Mongolia to again help Mongolian teachers and students improve their English fluency.

But all this is for anyone who wants to learn English. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, feel free to contact me on the Anglihel.com Facebook page.
