Stand Up Sit Down

by Fun Kids English

Let's move and learn! "Stand Up, Sit Down" is a fun actions song to get children moving and learning. In this song we'll learn and do: stand up, sit down, turn around, put one hand up, put one hand down, put two hands up, put two hands down, jump, hop, run and stop. A very fun and energetic song that kids love!
