  • people: persons; men, women and children
  • body: the whole physical structure of a human or an animal
  • neck: the part of the body between the head and the shoulders
  • chest: the top part of the front of the body, between the neck and the stomach
  • abdomen: the part of the body below the chest that contains the stomach, bowels, etc.
  • hip: the area at either side of the body between the top of the leg and the waist; the joint at the top of the leg
  • genitals: a person’s sex organs that are outside their body
  • shin: the front part of the leg below the knee
  • male: belonging to the sex that does not give birth to babies
  • leg: one of the long parts that connect the feet to the rest of the body
  • groin: the part of the body where the legs join at the top including the area around the genitals
  • nipple: either of the two small round dark parts on a person’s chest
  • head: the part of the body on top of the neck containing the eyes, nose, mouth and brain
  • breast: either of the two round soft parts at the front of a woman’s body that produce milk when she has had a baby
  • waist: the area around the middle of the body between the ribs and the hips, often narrower than the areas above and below
  • navel: the small hollow part or lump in the middle of the stomach where the umbilical cord was cut at birth
  • forearm: the part of the arm between the elbow and the wrist
  • thigh: the top part of the leg between the knee and the hip
  • knee: the joint between the top and bottom parts of the leg where it bends in the middle
  • foot: the lowest part of the leg, below the ankle, on which a person or an animal stands
  • female: of the sex that can lay eggs or give birth to babies
  • shoulder: either of the two parts of the body between the top of each arm and the neck
  • arm: either of the two long parts that stick out from the top of the body and connect the shoulders to the hands
  • elbow: the joint between the upper and lower parts of the arm where it bends in the middle
  • wrist: the joint between the hand and the arm
  • hand: the part of the body at the end of the arm, including the fingers and thumb
  • ankle: the joint connecting the foot to the leg
  • nape: the back of the neck
  • back: the part of the human body that is on the opposite side to the chest, between the neck and the tops of the legs
  • armpit: the part of the body under the arm where it joins the shoulder
  • small of the back
  • buttock: either of the two round soft parts at the top of a person’s legs
  • calf: the back part of the leg between the ankle and the knee
  • heel: bottom part of the foot
